Marketing

Marketing Coordinator

TENH100 is a Cambodian e-commerce marketplace connecting merchants to customers for goods and services. Based in Phnom Penh and building from the ground up, we need a fresh, creative marketer who is comfortable with ambiguity, shifting priorities, and the realities of an early-stage startup.

  • Design and execute campaigns across social media for merchant and customer acquisition.
  • Manage content calendars, campaign reporting, ad budgets, and performance optimization.
  • Write promotional copy in both English and Khmer and coordinate closely with graphic designers.
  • Test new channels such as TikTok, influencers, and local communities, then scale what works.
  • Support merchant onboarding, partnerships, and operational tasks as the business evolves.

What we're looking for: bilingual English and Khmer communication, real campaign experience, comfort with data and analytics tools, and a high tolerance for unstructured, fast-changing SOPs.

Phnom Penh Full-time
